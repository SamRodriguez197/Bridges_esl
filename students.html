Program Overview

Bridges ESL is a non-profit organization that provides free small-group English tutoring to immigrants and visiting internationals in the Yale and New Haven communities. The program offers several levels of instruction depending on each student's needs.

Level System

We offer four levels of instruction to meet students at their current English proficiency:

Level 1 - Survival
For learners who know the alphabet and can write some basic words.
Level 2 - Beginner/Low Intermediate
For learners who know simple grammar and can write sentences.
Level 3 - High Intermediate
For learners who can write a paragraph explaining their thoughts and opinions even though there might be some errors.
Level 4 - Low Advanced
For learners who can write an essay explaining their point of view.
Level 5 - High Advanced
For learners who are able to give full professional presentations and explain abstract views.

Learning Experience

Most sessions include the introduction of new grammar or conversation skills in small groups. Depending on the level and interest of the class, lessons may also feature field trips, community meals, or cultural discussions designed to promote practical language use and cultural exchange.
