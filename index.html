Building Bridges Since 2003

Bridges ESL is a non-profit volunteer organization that provides free small-group English tutoring to immigrants and internationals in the New Haven community.

Our mission is to help adult learners acquire the English skills necessary to confidently navigate family, work, and community life in the United States. Through personalized lessons and community support, we've been proudly serving New Haven for over two decades.
